Get Involed with Papertalk

Papertalk is designed and developed in the open, and we'd love to have your help. The contributors hang out in our Google+ community, so that's the best place to find us.

Also check out the github repo, especially the current issue list and wiki.

Although Papertalk is open, we focus on user experience to a degree more typically associated with consumer web services. In these early days, the core contributors will keep a firm hand on the rudder to make sure that the project gets a solid start.
